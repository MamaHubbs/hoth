Grand Design Imagine XLS 22BHE – Our Two-Year Owner Review

This page accompanies our YouTube walkthrough of our Imagine XLS 22BHE.

Why We Chose the 22BHE

We picked the Grand Design Imagine XLS 22BHE after a lot of searching, comparing, and crawling through trailers—and now that we’re two years into ownership, we can honestly say it’s everything we hoped it would be.

We wanted a couple’s trailer that was half-ton towable but also still had space for our adult kids, our mid-sized dog, a cat if we want, and enough room to work remotely. Being able to reach the fridge and bathroom with the slide in was a must-have. The 22BHE checks all of those boxes without feeling cramped.

Comfort & Layout

One of the best parts is the headroom. At 5'10" and 6'3", we don’t have to duck through doorways or squeeze into a tiny shower. The layout feels open, and the kitchen counter space and cabinets are surprisingly generous for a trailer this size.

Construction & Build Quality

After looking at many other RVs, the construction on Grand Design stood out. The laminated aluminum framed walls give the trailer a solid feel, and the wood truss roof is sturdy, quiet, and holds temperature well.

The one-piece TPO roof, fully enclosed heated underbelly, and NitroFill tires give peace of mind in cold weather and long towing days. Even the aluminum entry steps feel like a real upgrade. The ductless flooring is huge for keeping dirt and dog hair out of vents.

Comfort in All Seasons

The 4-Seasons Protection Package really works—this trailer stays comfortable in all sorts of weather.

We’re lukewarm on the tankless water heater (we miss being able to run the pump off electric), but it heats water fast.

The double axle and integrated anti-lock brakes make towing feel stable and controlled.

Solar Power & the 12V Fridge

We love camping off-grid, so the factory solar setup was a big selling point.

One of the biggest wins is the 12V refrigerator. It runs off solar or battery, stays on while traveling, and has more usable interior space because the components are smaller. Huge win-win.

Storage & Exterior Features

The pass-through storage is fantastic. Grand Design cuts the openings larger than most trailers and routes wiring out of the way so the whole space is usable.

The accessory hitch also gives us easy space for extra gear.

Final Thoughts

If you’re watching our walkthrough video, we hope this write-up helps explain why we chose the 22BHE and why we’re still so happy with it two years later.
